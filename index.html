Cyber Security

In the digital age, cybersecurity has become an increasingly important issue. As more and more of our personal and sensitive information is stored online, it is essential that we take steps to protect ourselves from cyber threats such as hacking, identity theft, and cyberbullying. This can involve using strong passwords, being cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments,and using security software such as antivirus programs and firewalls.

Cybersecurity Law and Policy:

This course covers the legal and policy aspects of cybersecurity, including privacy laws, intellectual property, and international regulations.

Digital Forensics:

This course focuses on the collection, analysis, and preservation of digital evidence to investigate cybercrime and other computer-related incidents.

Cloud Security:

This course focuses on securing cloud-based systems and data, including securing data storage and applications in the cloud.

Incident Response:

This course provides training on how to respond to cyber incidents, including how to identify, contain, and recover from cyber attacks.
