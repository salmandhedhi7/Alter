Honda CG 125 Specification


The Honda CG 125 is a kick-start motorcycle that is known for its good performance and durability. It comes with a roller chain and has the standard features of CG 125 without much change over the years.

Bike Specification

Engine 4-Stroke OHV Air Cooled
Bore And Stroke 56.5 x 49.5 mm
Starting Kick Start
Final Drive Roller Chain
Transmission 4 Speed Constant Mesh
Dimension(LxWxH) 1912 x 735 x 1026 mm
Seat Height 764 mm
Ground Clearance 132 mm
Petrol Capacity 9.2 Liters (Reserve: 2 Liters)
Wheel Base 1204 mm
Tire at Front 2.50 – 18 (4 PR)
Tire at Back 3.00 – 17 (6 PR)
Suspension Front Telescopic Fork 103 mm Travel
Suspension Back Swing Arm 68 mm Travel
Dry Weight 100kg
